## What's all this fuss about AI-written content on Hacker News?

so, this week, ~~I wrote~~ AI wrote [a blog post](/blog/no-competition-red-flag-solopreneur-validated-market) for me on Monday Nov 10th.

I didn't submit it right away the same day, because I wanted a steady momentum from other social media platforms first.

### The Unexpected Viral Moment

then on Thursday evening (my evening, EST/PST's morning)

it got submitted and over the first half an hour of submission, it got a few upvotes and a few comments

it was a clear and early sign of going "viral" on HN

I say viral in quotes because I have never had a viral moments on HN, never before, not at this scale

### Why This Matters to Small Creators

now you might be a content creator who gets these numbers for lunch but for me it's a different story. for me these numbers matter

cause my normal numbers are usually around 200 impressions (from 15k linkedin followers), maybe 1-2 likes and zero comments (from my 150 X followers), and generally, I never got any more than 2 upvotes for my personal blog posts on HN before

so you can imagine this is a big deal for me. not for Elon Musk and others of course but I'm no billionaire, hell I'm not even a millionaire

now, you can go ahead and read the subsmission for yourself, it's at the bottom of this page in the references section

but here's my line of thoughts towards those folks who were calling me out, making me look like a villain

here's the unfiltered, non-AI generated format of how I would like to speak to those individuals who were so blatantly against my submission

## My raw, non-AI and unfiltered response to HN folks

### The Moral High Ground Problem

what's all this moral high ground everyone takes?

English is not my first language and HN is, let's face it, primarily for US and English speaking folks

I, just like any other small creator, am just trying to submit my website, get a juicy backlink and increase my chance of survival over millions of billions of other webpages across the web, and hopefully get some discussions going, learn something and ideally I may even get some email subscribers to my newsletter

which I DID

### The Numbers Don't Lie

now, let's talk numbers before I go deeper.

in less than 12hr from submission, I got nearly 1200+ unique visitors to my website, [meysam.io](https://meysam.io), I even got 100% traffic increase to my other OSS project [awesome-directories.com](https://awesome-directories.com) in the same timeframe

that is not prejudice or bias, that is just pure data-driven outcome, or decision making framework if I may; out of those 1.2k, 4m avg duration spent on the website and 93% bounce rate from all around the glob and not just the USA (48%)

I even got two new subscribers to my newsletter, which is the whole fucking point of writing and building in public in the first place

now I am not denying that I didn't write the blog post entirely from AI

I use AI daily for everything, development or marketing

and I am not shy nor am I going to apologize to no-fucking-body

I mean, technology is here to make our lives easier, more efficient and faster

does anyone blame students for using calculator for performing their number churn?

how about business worlds where we use tech everyday to speed up the revenue?

I was using C++ back in the day, then python and then Go, and now AI.
are you gonna cry about it? grow the fuck up.

I am using AI. sue me.

### The AI-Written Content Backlash

why is there so many backlashes? what's the hate towards AI-written content?

do you not see AI blog post generator these days popping out like mushrooms?
I know a handful few and I'm not gonna drop names here but do you go out dismissing their entire business model because you don't like AI-fucking-writing-style?

### It's Well-Written, Period

I mean, the blog post is very well-written, [read it](/blog/no-competition-red-flag-solopreneur-validated-market)

it is punchy, very engaging, it is breathtaking and you almost do not blink while you're reading it

it is a very well-focused, high-engagement piece of article; a great storytelling, a solid narrative

and it is not something that's fake or anything

it's coming from my own raw thoughts

I fed the AI with my thoughts and asked it to ask me questions, the answers of which will shape a high-engaging blog post with my tone, with my authenticitiy, with my own personal and emotional twist

### The Time Reality Check

and I am darn happy with the result because it has been written in a way I couldn't have done myself in a hundred years

### First Viral Moment Ever
